WORK

Verber

Verber is an application based on experiencing historical sites and spaces through sound simulation. You can choose from the available catalogue, or you can add to the catalogue by creating your own space with an available Impulse Response.The app connects to your microphone and gives you an impression of being in a space while being at your computer.The application also offers the users to upload Ambient/Field Recording Data related to the place.

Sounds of the Future Past

Generative music from the future as heard on the analog devices of the past. This installation uses a self-generating sound device made in Max/Msp recorded on a akai gx4000D reel-reel tape machine

Songs for a tired city

A sonic exploration of New Delhi, and attempt at lifting a few of the cities layers and listening to what they reveal. Showcased at Goethe Institue, New Delhi.

P.

Ambient Audio Recordings off the streets of Kolkata/Glasgow/Utrecht converted into midi data and then used to generate piano compositions using Ableton Live.
